# 🧘 How to Regulate Your Emotions

- 👀 **Notice** your feelings without judgment.  
- 🧘‍♂️ **Pause and breathe** before reacting.  
- 🗣️📝🏃‍♀️🧘 **Use healthy outlets**: talk, write, exercise, or meditate.  
- 🙏 **Practice gratitude** and mindfulness daily.  
- 🧑‍⚕️ **Seek professional help** if emotions feel unmanageable.
